About the Golf Course

Our 18-hole golf course offers guests an outside activity for players of all skill levels, whether its playing a full round of 18 holes or just practicing at the driving range there is something on offer for a variety of ages.

Course Layout

Explore the layout of our beautifully designed golf course, featuring strategically placed hazards, lush fairways, and perfectly maintained greens. Spanning over 3 acres the course features both the options for 9 and 18 hole games with a 9 hole pitch and put as well.

  • 18 holes across 7,200 yards
  • Par 72 course
  • Signature 7th hole with island green
  • Challenging sand bunkers and water features

Course Highlights

Hole 1 - Difficult Par 4

A scenic opening hole with a dogleg right and water along the left side. Requires accuracy off the tee to avoid the water hazard.

Hole 7 - Signature Hole - Par 3

Our signature hole with an island green. Precision is key for this short but challenging hole.

Hole 18 - Par 5

A long finishing hole with a wide fairway and strategically placed bunkers. Great for a dramatic finish.
